## Animation Timing

### Progress Bar Fill
- **Easing:** `cubic-bezier(0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1)` (Bouncy)
- **Duration:** 0.6 seconds
- **Effect:** Smooth bounce when percentage updates

### Modal Appearance  
- **Easing:** `ease` (Linear)
- **Duration:** 0.5 seconds
- **Effect:** Fade in and slide up from bottom

### Button States
- **Hover:** Immediate color change
- **Selected:** Immediate class toggle
- **Transition:** 0.3 second smooth color change
